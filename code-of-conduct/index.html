Code of Conduct

A delegate or delegation will be disqualified if they do not refrain from the following: 

Physical Violence

Physical violence, use of foul language and misbehavior with JT Sportsfest management or other participants.

Damaging School Property

Damaging school property or entering any restricted areas.

Weapons

Bringing any weapon on the venue(s).

Unfair Means or Cheating

Using unfair means to gain advantage over the other participants in an attempt to win a competition.

Smoking or Drinking

Use of cigarettes, vapes, E-cigarettes, alcohol or any other intoxicant on the campus. Any vaping device/E-cigarette will be confiscated and will not be returned.

Noise and Disturbance

Producing excessive noise or disturbance on campus.

Offensive Material

Portraying of violence, profanity, drug abuse and any other offensive material.

Damaging Equipment

Misusing or damaging any of the equipment provided by the management.

Harassment

Any attempt of harassing or offending any other delegate physical or verbally
